Getting started

  • Open the app and go to Settings
  • Choose your AI provider — Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google
  • Paste your API key. It's stored locally and never sent to Nohi servers
  • Click Chat and ask about your orders, inventory, or customers
  • Explore built-in Skills or create your own in ~/.nohi/skills/
